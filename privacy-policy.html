Privacy Policy for Kudoro

Effective date: 16.02.2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Kudoro app, which is developed and maintained by Klippros Studios AB. Kudoro does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to its own servers. Most processing happens on the user's device. Certain subscription-related data is processed by third-party providers (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat) as described below. The app is provided “as is” and used entirely at your own risk.

Kudoro does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any data-collecting SDKs, except as described in this policy for Strava and subscription management via RevenueCat. For questions or concerns, please contact kudoro@klippros.com.

What information does Kudoro obtain and how is it used?

Kudoro does not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to provide its features. Most processing happens on your device. Subscription-related information is processed by third-party providers as described in the "Subscriptions and RevenueCat" section.

The app may access the following types of data:

Media library

Kudoro may request access to your device's media library (photos and videos) so you can create overlays with your own content. This data is only accessed with your permission, processed entirely on your device, and never transmitted to Kudoro servers or third parties. Any content you generate (e.g., overlays) is stored only on your device; it is up to you to share it.

Data from Strava

Strava activity data, profile information, and related data are accessed only with your explicit authorization via the official Strava API. Kudoro has access to both public and private activities from your Strava account when you choose to connect.

All Strava data is processed on-device and never transmitted to Kudoro servers or third parties. Users may revoke access at any time in Kudoro's settings or via Strava settings.

It is in the nature of the generated overlays that they include activity data. These overlays are stored only on your device. Choosing who to share them with is entirely up to you, for example, through social media, messaging apps, or any other method.

Subscriptions and RevenueCat

Kudoro uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. The following data is processed by RevenueCat and Apple/Google to provide subscription functionality:

  • Subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Renewal date and purchase history for the Pro tier
  • Device or RevenueCat identifiers used to link purchases
  • Locale and currency information for display purposes

Subscriptions are auto-renewing unless canceled by the user via Apple or Google. Kudoro does not access or store your payment information, email, or personal account details.

The app displays subscription status, renewal date, and provides a Restore Purchases button. Users cannot cancel subscriptions within the app but are given instructions for managing subscriptions through Apple or Google accounts.

RevenueCat processes subscription data to manage purchases, restore access, synchronize subscription status across devices, and provide subscription reporting to the developer. Kudoro does not use advertising, behavioral tracking, or user profiling analytics.

RevenueCat and the respective app store may generate and store pseudonymous customer identifiers (such as an App User ID), country information, subscription history, transaction dates, and total spend related to your subscription. Kudoro may access this subscription-related information through the RevenueCat dashboard for revenue reporting and limited customer support purposes.

RevenueCat and the respective app stores act as independent data controllers for subscription processing.

These records are pseudonymous and do not include your name, email address, payment details, or other directly identifying information. Kudoro does not combine this data with other datasets and cannot directly identify individual users.

Location data

Kudoro does not use any location data whatsoever.

Local Storage

Kudoro stores app settings and cached data locally on your device to improve performance. This data never leaves your device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by Kudoro?

Kudoro does not share any information with third parties except as described in the Third-Party Services section below.

Third-Party Services

Kudoro interacts with external services for essential features:

Strava API: Accessed only if you choose to connect your Strava account. All data is processed on-device and is never transmitted to Kudoro servers.

RevenueCat: Used for subscription management. RevenueCat processes subscription status, purchase history, pseudonymous identifiers, country information, transaction dates, and total spend to enable subscription functionality and reporting. Kudoro does not receive payment details, names, email addresses, or other directly identifying information.

Kudoro does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any other data-collecting SDKs.

Your Rights & Opt-Out

Since Kudoro does not collect or transmit personal data to its own servers, you may:

  • Revoke Strava access at any time through the app settings or via Strava settings
  • Revoke media library access in your device settings
  • Restore purchases using the RevenueCat button
  • Cancel subscriptions via Apple or Google account settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

Legal Basis for Processing

Kudoro primarily processes data locally on your device. Subscription-related data is processed externally by Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat for payment handling and subscription management. Kudoro does not receive directly identifying personal information from these providers. The legal basis for any processing is your explicit consent when you grant media library access, connect your Strava account, or use subscriptions. You may withdraw this consent at any time by revoking permissions or uninstalling the app.

GDPR (European Economic Area and United Kingdom)

If you are located in the EEA or the United Kingdom:

  • Kudoro does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to its own servers. Subscription-related data is processed by Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat in accordance with their respective privacy policies.
  • You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your data. You can exercise these rights by revoking Strava and media library access, deleting generated content, and uninstalling the app.
  • You also have the right to object to processing, withdraw consent at any time, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For requests or complaints, contact kudoro@klippros.com. You may also contact your local data protection authority (supervisory authority).

California Privacy Rights

California Residents – Your Rights under CCPA, CPRA, and CalOPPA

If you are a California resident, the following applies to your use of Kudoro:

1. Personal Information We Access

  • Media library content (photos/videos) on your device, if you grant access.
  • Strava activity data, profile information, and related data, if you connect your Strava account.
  • Subscription status, renewal date, and purchase history via RevenueCat.

Media and Strava data are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to Kudoro servers. Subscription-related data is processed by Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat to enable purchases and subscription management.

2. Your Rights under CCPA/CPRA

California residents have the right to:

  • Know: Request information about the personal information we access.
  • Delete: Request deletion of personal information. For Kudoro, this means revoking Strava access, revoking media library access, deleting generated content, or uninstalling the app.
  • Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: Kudoro does not sell or share personal information. Should this ever change, you would have the option to opt out.
  • Non-Discrimination: You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

3. How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact kudoro@klippros.com with your name, email, and a clear description of your request.

4. CalOPPA – Do Not Track (DNT)

Kudoro does not track users across apps or websites. If your device sends a Do Not Track signal, Kudoro does not collect personal information in response.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this California-specific section from time to time. The latest version is always available at https://kudoro.cc/privacy-policy. Continued use of Kudoro constitutes acceptance of any updates.

Children's Privacy

Kudoro does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If a child connects a Strava account, access may be revoked or the app uninstalled. Parents or guardians may contact kudoro@klippros.com to request deletion.

Security

Strava data accessed by Kudoro is stored locally on your device. Subscription-related data is stored and processed by Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat. Kudoro only retrieves subscription status information as needed. Kudoro does not transmit media or Strava data externally. Subscription-related data is transmitted directly to Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat for payment and subscription processing. You are responsible for securing your device using passcodes, biometrics, or encryption.
